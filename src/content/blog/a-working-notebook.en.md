This site started as a way to keep two things side by side: the research I do on water and plastic, and the photographs I make in the same places. A journal felt like the missing third piece. It is the part where I get to slow down and write about why any of it matters to me.

I have just finished an MSc in Urban Environmental Management at Wageningen. Most of what I produced over the last two years lives in formal documents: a thesis, reports, a transcript. Useful, but quiet about the thinking behind them. Here I want to leave a looser record. Field notes, half formed ideas, a photo that taught me something about a place.

So this is what you can expect in this corner of the site:

- short notes from the water and microplastics work, written for a curious reader rather than an examiner
- the occasional photo essay from the four countries I have studied and worked in
- things I am reading, and questions I have not answered yet

I will write in both English and Chinese. Not every post will exist in both languages, and that is fine. If a post has a translation, you will see a link to it at the top.

Thanks for reading. If something here sparks a question or a collaboration, the contact section is one scroll away.
